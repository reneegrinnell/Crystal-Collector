Crystal Collector

A Blingy Math Game

How to Play:
At the start of the game, a random number ("Target Value") will appear and each of the four crystal buttons below will be assigned different random values. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.

Win a round by matching your total score to the randomly selected target value. If the total score exceeds the target value, you lose!
